FAQs

What is CleanWide?

CleanWide is a web-based platform for efficient waste management, aimed at both individuals and businesses. It allows users to monitor and coordinate collections, generate reports, and receive personalized recommendations to promote sustainable practices. Each segment (individuals and businesses) has features tailored to their needs.

How can I contribute to sustainability through this platform?

Through the platform, you can receive personalized recommendations on how to reduce waste and improve your recycling practices. Additionally, the gamified system and tracking of sustainable achievements will encourage you to implement more responsible actions, such as reducing, reusing, and recycling.

Can I access detailed reports on waste management?

Yes, the platform offers detailed reports on the amount of waste generated, classified by type and period. These reports allow for efficient monitoring and informed decision-making to optimize waste management.

Are there automation options for waste collection?

Yes, CleanWide offers businesses the ability to automate waste collection by integrating automated collection services. This makes it easy to schedule and efficiently manage collection tasks without constant manual intervention.
